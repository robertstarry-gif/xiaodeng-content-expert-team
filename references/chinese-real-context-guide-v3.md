# Chinese Real Context Guide V3

Use this guide when writing for Chinese readers, especially when the user says the draft feels AI-like, too logical, too clever, or not like something a person would actually write.

## The Core Test

A sentence is not good just because it is neat. It must pass three checks:

1. **Can the reader recognize the situation?**
2. **Does the example happen before the conclusion becomes big?**
3. **Would a normal person accept the wording, not just admire the rhetoric?**

If any answer is no, rewrite from scene, action, and consequence.

## Openings

Prefer an opening that sounds like a reader's real question or moment.

Weak:

> 周末最累的，不是没休息，是一直在判断怎么休息。

Better:

> 好不容易熬到周末，本想好好放松一下，为什么却更累了？

Why: the better line starts with lived expectation and a familiar contradiction. It does not force an abstract thesis before the reader has entered the scene.

## Examples Before Conclusions

Do not explain the whole article through a metaphor before giving a normal example.

Weak:

> 脑子里那些选项挤在一起，像一群人堵在门口，每个都说自己很重要。

Better:

> 你本来只是想挑个电影，结果又想起衣服没洗、朋友的消息没回、外卖还没点。手机翻了半小时，真正开始休息的那一刻反而一直没来。

Why: the better version names ordinary choices and shows the delay. It does not make the reader decode a decorative picture.

## Watch These AI-Like Logic Shapes

Rewrite when the sentence relies on form more than truth:

- `不是 A，而是 B`
- `真正重要的不是 A，而是 B`
- `很多人不是被 A 占走，而是被 B 占走`
- `这背后，其实是...`
- `它不只是 X，更是 Y`
- `在某种意义上`
- `我们需要重新理解`

These forms are allowed only when the contrast is concrete and useful. Otherwise, turn the idea into a scene.

## The Metaphor Rule

Use a metaphor only if it does real work.

Ask:

- Is the comparison immediately understandable?
- Does every important part map to the actual situation?
- Would the sentence still be clear if the metaphor were removed?
- Is the metaphor something the reader has experienced?

If the metaphor is only there to sound literary, remove it.

## Concrete Material Bank

Use at least one type of concrete material early:

- a place: kitchen, subway, office desk, bedroom, clinic hallway, supermarket aisle;
- an action: checking a price, washing a bowl, deleting a chat draft, refreshing a page;
- an object: receipt, cold rice, folding chair, bank statement, medicine box, contract clause;
- a time pressure: before work, after dinner, Sunday night, payment deadline, test result day;
- a small decision: call or not call, buy or wait, cook or order, apologize or stay silent.

## Domain Texture

### Food / Lifestyle

Use sensory and practical details: heat, smell, waiting time, cleanup, portion, leftovers, grocery habits, food safety. Avoid fake "I tested" claims.

### Relationships / Emotion

Use ordinary messages, delayed replies, awkward silence, boundary-setting, family scenes, and specific misunderstandings. Do not diagnose another person's mind.

### Finance / Investing

Use assumptions, risk, time horizon, cash flow, fees, liquidity, and uncertainty. Do not turn education into buy/sell instruction.

### Legal

Use jurisdiction, date, document type, process stage, evidence, deadlines, and "what this does not decide." Do not provide personalized legal strategy.

### Medical / Health

Use symptom literacy, appointment context, uncertainty, red flags, and clinician boundaries. Do not diagnose or prescribe.

### Fiction

Use desire, obstacle, setting, object, gesture, silence, and consequence. Do not explain the theme before the scene breathes.

### Brand Advertising

Use pain, proof, objection, moment of use, audience language, and conversion path. Do not claim outcomes without evidence.

## Rewrite Moves

- Abstract noun -> action: "选择压力" becomes "电影没选完，又切去看外卖。"
- Grand claim -> small consequence: "重新掌控生活" becomes "这顿饭至少让今天不是被外卖列表决定的。"
- Decorative metaphor -> ordinary chain: "选项堵门" becomes "洗衣、回消息、点外卖、补觉一起挤进脑子里。"
- General crowd -> one reader: "很多人" becomes "一个忙到周五晚上才松口气的人。"
- Slogan ending -> closed thought: "生活会因此改变" becomes "它改变不了一周，但能把这一晚往回拿一点。"

## Final Chinese Reality Pass

Before final delivery, mark any line that:

- could appear in any topic;
- sounds like a speech rather than an article;
- uses a metaphor no one would naturally say;
- explains a feeling before showing it;
- turns a small point into a life philosophy too early.

Rewrite those lines in ordinary Chinese.
